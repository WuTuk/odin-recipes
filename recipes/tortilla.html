Tortilla de patatas

Description:

Typical spanish food, easy to do, but most important, super delicious!

Ingredients:

  • 12 eggs
  • 500g of potatoes
  • 2 onion
  • olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • Sicilian sea salt and black pepper, to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat pan with olive oil
  2. Fry the potatoes with the onion and garlic.
  3. When brown, put in a bowl with the battered eggs and let it there for a couple mins.
  4. Put it back in the pan, and when done from one side, flip it over with the help of a plate
