Spaghetti jalapeño

Description:

Spicy and sweet comfort food for heat lovers!

Ingredients:

  • 375g box noodles
  • 450g cheddar cheese
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of fresh corn
  • 2 Jalapeño
  • 2 tbsp finely cut basil
  • Sicilian sea salt and black pepper, to taste

Steps:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions; drain, reserving 1/3 cup (75 mL) of the boiled pasta water.
  2. Meanwhile, melt 2 tbsp (30 mL) butter in large skillet set over medium heat; cook onion, garlic, and 1/4 tsp (1 mL) of both salt and pepper for 3 to 5 minutes or until softened.
  3. Stir in flour; cook for 1 to 2 minutes or until smooth. Slowly whisk in milk. Bring to boil, whisking constantly. Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer for 8 to 10 minutes or until mixture is slightly thickened; remove from heat. Stir in cheese until melted.
  4. Meanwhile, melt remaining butter in separate skillet set over medium-high heat; cook corn and jalapeños for 5 to 8 minutes or until lightly charred.
  5. Stir spaghetti, corn mixture and jalapeños into cheese sauce until well coated. If sauce is too thick, stir in enough of the reserved boiled pasta water for desired consistency. Garnish with cilantro.

    6. Tips:

    • Substitute parsley or basil for cilantro if desired.
    • For a less-spicy dish, use only 1 jalapeño pepper
